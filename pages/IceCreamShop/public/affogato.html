Affogato, the ice cream and its combinations.

By Admin / 10st december, 2021

The ice cream can generate impressive combinations, either with fruits or different products, they can be so interesting unions, that with the passage of time, different methods and mixtures have been adapted, then you will know the Affogato.

What is Affogato?

The affogato is a classic of Italian desserts, it is the mixture of coffee, liquor or hot chocolate with an amount of ice cream similar to or greater than the liquid, hence its name, which in English translates as "Ahogato". The contrast of temperature and flavors generate these interesting mixtures. Coffee can be combined with ice cream, the recommended flavors are almost always vanilla, chocolate, mango and blackberry, but it all depends on your taste.

In the case of chocolate its flavor is more versatile, but in the same way it can be combined with ice cream, in this case, the ice cream flavors can vary and although it is not recommended to mix with chocolate ice cream, you have a diversity of flavors, such as hazelnut, soursop, strawberry, among others.

While with liquor, it will depend on the type of liquor and the ice cream, for example, a mixture that we recommend is coffee liquor or better known as Amaretto, this, as well as normal coffee, can be mixed with the aforementioned flavors, but you can add mint flavor. But if we are talking about stronger liqueurs, exotic fruit ice creams will be your best combination.

Affogato as we prepare it.

    Ingredients and utensils:

  • 2 scoops of mango ice cream. (Or the flavor of your choice, see our product menu ).
  • 1 shot of hot espresso coffee.
  • Nuts or almonds (to taste).
  • Small marshmallows (to taste).
  • 1 glass cup previously chilled in the freezer.


    Preparation:

  • Place the ice cream into the chilled glass, then pour the hot coffee shor.
  • Add the amount of nuts or almonds as needed.
  • And finally we can add small marshmallows, for the affogato this ingredient is not necessary, but we believe it goes very well with its preparation.
